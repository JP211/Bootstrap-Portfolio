About Me

I am so excited to be an SMU Full Stack program graduate. Since graduation I have ventured into the world and I am now developing applications of my own so that I may continue to progress as a developer.

The most valuable advice I have ever received was to “always be learning and always be coding”. I consider myself a lifelong learner and I am passionate about discovering new technologies and seeing ideas come to life.

I have a passion for developing applications and seeing projects go from the early concept phase all the way to launch and final product. While my favorite part of the process involves the backend (especially mongo / databases) I also enjoy the visual aspects of the front end.

I am currently pursuing my Bachelor of Science in Computer Information Systems / Computer Science at Columbia College online.
